Tumekie Blackwood (D.O.B. July 19,1999) was born in Kingston, Jamaica. He attended Camperdown High School where he completed 7 years while playing cricket, captaining both the School's Challenge Quiz team and the Manning cup team. He graduated in 2018 with his diploma and received a soccer scholarship to study Engineering at Northwest College. He graduated Northwest College in 2021 where he received his Associate Degree in Engineering and Mathematics. He later transfered to Southwest Baptist University where he is aiming to get his bachelors in Computer Science and Mathematics while competing in the GLVC division 2 soccer conference.
